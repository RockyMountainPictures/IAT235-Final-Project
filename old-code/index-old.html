about us

The Carnaval de Québec is a vibrant and enchanting winter festival held in Québec City, Canada. We bring a diverse range of activities, parades, and cultural events that celebrate the spirit of Québécois culture and the beauty of the winter season.

our vision

The 67th Carnival is a continuation of the new vision, but adapted to the exceptional situation due to the pandemic. It will focus its efforts on the traditional and essential elements that have always been part of its history: to break the gloom of winter and stimulate the local economy.

meet bonhomme

Bonhomme is the iconic snowman mascot representative of Carnaval de Québec who has become a symbol of the carnival, embodying the spirit of the festive event. Born in 1954, Bonhomme holds tradition and heritage for he represents Québec’s winter culture and identity through his presence and physical appearance. With Bonhomme at the carnival, he helps to unite the community and attract visitors from around the world as he is loved by all!

Visitor's Guide

carnaval de quebec is one of the world’s largest winter events that celebrates the experience of canada and its winters. learn more about the mascots that represent the activities at the carnival through the interactive map or purchase your own physical effigy as a memoir of your experience at the carnival!

mascots

Bonhomme, alongside a few animals native to Québec, have been chosen to lead and represent the main activities at the carnival. Learn more about Bonhomme and his winter wonderland world, and his friends through the interactive map of the carnival!

effigies

This is an effigy, designed differently every year, and is your physical ticket to enter the winter carnival! The effigy acts as both a souvenir and a ticket, and can even get you discounts at hotels, restaurants, and a visit to the ice hotel! Attendees have the option to purchase and receive the souvenir effigy of the 69th edition of the carnival.

activities

Carnaval de Québec hosts a variety of different activities that attendees can partake in. from the food truck alley of mouth-watering dishes to the concert of fun and dance, it’s a nine-day event of endless fun and new experiences for both locals and tourists!
